Questions people ask

Do I need a Scrappy Stacks design plan to get a Launch Kit?

No — the Launch Kit stands on its own. Most people pair it with a design plan since the two together get you fully set up, brand and business both.

Will you actually file my LLC for me?

We walk you through the exact steps for your state and hand you the checklist. Filing itself is done through your state's site or a formation service, since that requires your signature and payment either way.

What if I already have some of this done?

Tell us what's already in place during intake and we'll skip it — you're not paying twice for a bank account you already opened.

How long does setup take?

Most Starter Kits are done within a week. Full Kits typically take one to two weeks, depending on how quickly we get what we need from you.
