Some free tools on the Site — like the Business Idea Generator — run in your browser and don't require you to submit personal information to use them.

4. Cookies

The Site uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site (for example, which pages are viewed and how visitors arrived). You can disable cookies through your browser settings; doing so may not affect your ability to use the Site, but it will limit the analytics data we receive.

5. How We Use Your Information

We don't send marketing emails unless you've asked to receive them, and we don't sell your personal information.
